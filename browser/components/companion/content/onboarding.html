What's new on

Pro Client

January 31, 2022

See "Running late" when it's most relevant

A bug fix removed the running late prompt on calendar events with no other guests, such as Focus Time.

Connect to Google services more seamlessly

Thanks to a bug fix, you can seamlessly connect Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Meet to Pro Client.

Fix for screen readers: Calendar menu

Screen readers can now announce the number of items in the calendar menu correctly.

All-day events removed from Companion

All-day events no longer take up as much space in your Companion. Find them in your Calendar view.

Close several views at once

Introducing multi-close on page views. Just right-click a view group and select Close View Group to save time.

Introducing Pro Client's new look and feel

To help instill calm while bringing your content front and center, the first visual styling updates have been made. Stay tuned for more robust visual styling soon.
